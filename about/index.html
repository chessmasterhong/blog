My programming experience started in 2006, when I self-taught PHP as my first programming language. Over time, I have learned several other programming languages, frameworks, and standard technologies. I have knowledge of PHP, JavaScript, C++, HTML, CSS, MySQL, and some Python.

In regards to operating systems, I have experience with both Windows and Linux, having nearly two decades of Windows experience (95, 98, Vista, XP, and 7), as well as several years of Linux (Ubuntu, Arch, and CentOS). Currently, I switch between Windows 7 and Arch Linux on a day-to-day basis.

Personally, I think programming is fun. The ability to solve problems and create solutions through code has always been fascinating to me. In my spare time, I am usually coding side projects or learning new technologies.
